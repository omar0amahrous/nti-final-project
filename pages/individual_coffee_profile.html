From Ethiopian Highlands to Your Cup

Grown at 2,000 meters above sea level in the Yirgacheffe region, these beans are cultivated by the Konga Cooperative, a group of 2,300 smallholder farmers who have been perfecting their craft for generations.

High Altitude Growing

The thin air and cool temperatures at 2,000m create dense, flavorful beans with bright acidity.

Sustainable Farming

Shade-grown under native trees, supporting biodiversity and soil health.

Fair Trade Partnership

Direct relationships ensure farmers receive premium prices for their exceptional quality.
