Omar's Story

A journey of passion, dedication, and the pursuit of the perfect cup

The Beginning

Omar's love affair with coffee began over 20 years ago in a small café in Egypt. As a young man working his first job, he was mesmerized by the ritual of coffee preparation and the way a perfect cup could transform someone's entire day.

"I remember the first time I tasted a perfectly brewed Egyptian coffee," Omar recalls. "The aroma, the texture, the complexity of flavors – it was like discovering a hidden world. I knew at that moment that I wanted to dedicate my life to understanding and perfecting this craft."

The Journey

After years of learning from master roasters across the Middle East and Europe, Omar decided to embark on his own journey. He traveled to coffee farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, Guatemala, and Costa Rica, learning about the entire process from seed to cup.

"Every origin tells a different story," Omar explains. "The volcanic soil of Guatemala gives the beans their distinctive spicy notes, while the high altitudes of Ethiopia create those bright, floral characteristics. Understanding these nuances became my obsession."

The Philosophy

"Coffee is not just a beverage; it's a moment of connection. Every cup should tell the story of its origin while creating new memories for those who drink it."

This philosophy drives everything Omar does. From the careful selection of beans to the precise roasting process, every decision is made with the goal of creating an exceptional experience for coffee lovers.

Sustainable Sourcing

Direct relationships with farmers ensure fair prices and sustainable practices that benefit both the environment and local communities.

Artisanal Roasting

Small-batch roasting using traditional methods combined with modern precision to bring out the unique characteristics of each bean.

Passion for Quality

Every step of the process is guided by an unwavering commitment to quality and the belief that great coffee can brighten anyone's day.

Streak Café Today

Today, Streak Café represents the culmination of Omar's journey and passion. Located in the heart of the city, the café serves as both a coffee shop and a testament to the art of coffee making.

"When people walk into Omar Café, I want them to feel the passion and care that goes into every cup," Omar says. "From our carefully curated selection of single-origin beans to our house blends, each coffee tells a story of its origin and our commitment to excellence."
