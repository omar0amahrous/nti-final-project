Omar's Pro Tips

Essential knowledge for brewing the perfect cup

Fresh Beans Matter

Always use coffee beans that have been roasted within 2-4 weeks. The roast date should be clearly marked on the package.

Proper Ratios

Use a digital scale to measure your coffee and water. The golden ratio is typically 1:15 to 1:17 (coffee to water).

Water Temperature

Optimal water temperature is between 195-205°F (90-96°C). Too hot burns the coffee, too cool under-extracts.

Grind Size

Match your grind size to your brewing method. Coarse for French press, medium for pour-over, fine for espresso.

Timing is Key

Each brewing method has its optimal extraction time. Stick to recommended times for the best flavor balance.

Water Quality

Use filtered water when possible. Poor water quality can negatively impact even the best coffee beans.
